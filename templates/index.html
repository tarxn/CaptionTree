WHY USE CAPTIONS ON YOUR VIDEOS?

Adding manual captions to your videos can be a critical part of your video content strategy.
• Your content will appear in more search results (Improved SEO).
• Provides clarity on technical terminology (automatic captions contain many errors on technical terms and grammar).
• Helpful for Non-English speakers and viewers with learning disabilities, attention deficits or autism.
• A video with captions has more views, watch time and retention than the same video without captions.
